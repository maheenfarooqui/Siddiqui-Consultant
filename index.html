Frequently ask

What documents are required for biometric verification?

You need your original CNIC, a recent passport-size photo,
and in some cases, a thumb impression on a stamp paper.

How long does it take to renew a CNIC?

On average, CNIC renewal through our service takes 3 to 5
working days, depending on NADRA processing time.

Can I get a stamp paper issued online?

Yes, we offer both physical and e-stamp paper issuance.
You can request it through WhatsApp or visit our office directly.
